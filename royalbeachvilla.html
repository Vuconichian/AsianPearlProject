Royal Beach Villa

Total Area: 900 sqm

Pool: 8x3 m

The ultimate accommodation is the Royal Beach Villa. Developed on two floors, the villa features on the ground floor two bedrooms, each with private bathrooms and closets, a lounge with private bar and kitchen, a private garden just outside the bathroom, large covered and open patios, a gazebo and a swimming pool. On the First floor is located another large guestroom with bathroom, massage area, lounge and walk-in closet.
