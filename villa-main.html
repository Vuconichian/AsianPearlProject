All villas have been built to harmoniously blend with the local environment, with carefully studied landscaping and perfectly crafted materials such as bamboo, natural stones and Langhi Langhi leaves. Designed in contemporary-style tropical architecture and interior design solutions, all villas enjoy an elegant, cozy and relaxed ambiance,a modern and comfortable style. Each one features two flat satellite TVs, a king size bed or an interconnecting room, walk-in and outdoor “under the stars” showers and a spacious bath tub able to accommodate two, a spacious dressing room and walk-in closet, room-controlled air conditioning and overhead fans, working desk and high-speed Wi-Fi available all over the Resort.
